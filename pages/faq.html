Frequently Asked Questions


  1. What does the over/under mean?

    Sportsbooks set a over/under or a "total", which is the predicted number of points the teams will score combined. You can bet on whether you think the actual outcome of the game will fall "under" the predicted "total" , or "over" it. If the "total" for the Lakers vs. Suns matchup closed at 188 and the Suns won 107-93, the combined 200 points would give "over" bettors a win.

  2. How do these bets work?

    Our app, like other forms of betting, features three forms of betting for available games. These bets are the spread, the moneyline, and the over/under.

    The easiest to understand is the moneyline. The moneyline is simply a bet on who will win the game. If you place money on a moneyline bet for a team to win, all you need to win the bet is for that team to win. Usually one team is favored to win the game. This is indicated on the primary game screen with a negative number located across from the team favored to win.

    The next type of bet is the spread. The spread is similar to the moneyline except it is less of a bet for a team to win as it is for a team to win by a margin. Again the spread is based around who is favored to win which is indicated on the primary game screen with a negative number located across from the team favored to win. The points spread is the value of that negative number by the favored team.

    In the associated example, the Baltimore Ravens are favored to win, and they are favored to win by 10.5 points. In order to win a spread bet on the Ravens, they would need to beat the Cleveland Browns by 10.5 points. In order to win a spread bet on the Browns, they would need to not lose to the Ravens by more than 10.5 points.

    The final bets are over/under bets. These bets are guesses as to whether or not the combined scores of both teams will reach a certain value. The value they need to equal is indicated next to the team that is not favored. A bet for the over means that both teams need to score over the indicated number in order to win the bet. A bet for the under means that both teams need to score under the indicated number in order to win the bet.
